If you're getting a new fence in Brisbane, you're almost certainly choosing between Colorbond® steel and timber. Both look great. Both do the job. So how do you decide?

We install both every week across Greater Brisbane, so here's our honest comparison.

## The Short Answer

**Choose Colorbond® if:** you want low maintenance, strong resale appeal, and a fence that handles Brisbane summers without a second thought.

**Choose timber if:** you love the natural look, you're doing a garden feature fence, or you want to match an existing timber fence.

## Colorbond® Steel Fencing

### Pros
- **No maintenance required** — doesn't rot, warp, or need painting
- **Handles Queensland weather** — engineered for Australian conditions including UV, heat, and humidity
- **22 colour options** — including Monument, Woodland Grey, Paperbark
- **Fast installation** — most jobs completed in 1–2 days
- **Great for privacy** — solid steel panels with no gaps
- **Strong resale value** — buyers know what they're getting

### Cons
- Less warmth and character than timber
- Can feel industrial in some settings (though modern profiles have changed this significantly)
- Doesn't suit all heritage-style homes

### Cost in Brisbane
Colorbond® fencing typically costs **$80–$130 per metre** supply and install in Brisbane, depending on height, terrain, and access.

---

## Timber Fencing

### Pros
- **Natural beauty** — nothing quite replicates the warmth of real timber
- **Highly customisable** — gap sizes, picket styles, heights, and finishes
- **Suits Queensland character homes** — especially Queenslanders and older homes with established gardens
- **Can be painted or stained** any colour you want
- **Softer aesthetic** — blends naturally into garden settings

### Cons
- **Requires regular maintenance** — paint or oil every few years
- **Susceptible to rot and termites** if not properly treated and maintained
- **Slightly shorter lifespan** than Colorbond® without maintenance
- Hardwood is more expensive upfront

### Cost in Brisbane
Treated pine fencing: **$70–$120 per metre**. Hardwood timber: **$110–$160 per metre**, depending on species and job complexity.

---

## What About Longevity?

| Material | Expected Lifespan | Maintenance Required |
|----------|------------------|----------------------|
| Colorbond® | 20–30+ years | Minimal (occasional wash) |
| Treated Pine | 10–15 years | Regular paint/oil |
| Hardwood | 20–30 years | Moderate paint/oil |

Over a 20-year period, Colorbond® is often the cheaper option when you factor in maintenance costs. But timber fans will tell you it's worth every cent of the upkeep.

---

## What Do Most Brisbane Homeowners Choose?

In our experience, about **70% of residential jobs** we quote end up going Colorbond®. It's lower maintenance, the colours are excellent, and it genuinely looks great on most Brisbane homes.

Timber is popular for:
- Front fences on character homes
- Garden screening and feature fences
- Properties where the neighbours have timber and you want to match
- Homeowners who simply love the natural look

---

## Which Should You Choose?

Ask yourself these questions:

1. **How much maintenance are you willing to do?** If the answer is "as little as possible" — go Colorbond®.
2. **What does your home look like?** Older Queenslander? Timber often suits it better. Modern home? Colorbond® usually wins.
3. **What's your budget long-term?** Colorbond® costs more upfront but less over time.
4. **Do you have an existing fence to match?** If so, match the material.
